Hangover Helper

Description

This is a breakfast casserole perfect for curing any degree of hangover; also perfect for brunches and potlucks!

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large potato, peeled and diced
  • ½ yellow onion, diced
  • 9 ounces chorizo sausage, crumbled
  • 8 eggs
  • ½ cup milk (Optional)
  • ½ red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 ½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch casserole dish with cooking spray.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add potato and onion; cook and stir until potatoes are golden brown and soft, about 10 minutes.
  3. Place chorizo in a nonstick skillet over medium heat; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, about 5 minutes.
  4. Whisk eggs with milk in a large bowl. Add red bell pepper, cooked potato and onion, and 1 cup Cheddar cheese to the egg mixture; pour into the casserole dish. Sprinkle 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese on top.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until firm and cheese is melted and starting to brown, about 30 minutes. Cool before serving, about 10 minutes.
